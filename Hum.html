Humanities

Humanities at Mass Academy is the English/History class we take. In humanities, we cover various different time periods and analyze the literature of those times. So far, we've studied the histories of various different time periods, from Ancient Egypt to modern day America. For more information, check out the timeline below.

Contemporary Works

We did a unit on contemporary works in which we studied various different culutural backgrounds. During this unit, we had multiple discussions about the difficulties that different immigrants/races faced with regards to settling into America (click to see reflection written about contemporary works)

1900s

During the study of the 1900's, we discussed authors such as Ray Bradbury and the effects of technology on society. Along with this, we wrote an essay reflecting on two of Bradbury's short stories. (Click to view the essay)

1800s

Over the summer, we had to read Walden, by Henry David Thoreau. Once school started, Ms. Small split us into groups and had us make skits, reflecting the ideals of Thoreau. (Click to see script for the skit)

Ancient Greece

During our Ancient Greece unit, we went over many texts relating to The Iliad. To be more specific, we read a chapter of The Iliad, as well as the play The Women of Troy. Our major assignment for this unit was to compare and contrast the two stories. (Click to view essay)

Ancient Egypt

In our Ancient Egypt unit, we talked about one specific city full of pyramid builders called Deir el-Medina, and discussed the literature found in the city. We then wrote an essay analyzing the poems found on pots from this anceint civilizations
